Colton In A Green Bronco

Digital Art, December 2022

Colton In A Green Bronco explores self-portraiture through digital medium, utilizing visual elements and symbols to reference my interest and hobby of cars. FUN FACT:Growing up as a little kid, my dream job was always to be a car designer. A dream of mine is to someday fix up and restore a '72 Ford Bronco.
